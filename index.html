About

I am the person who organizes the junk drawer and uses an obscene amount of pens to color-code a to-do list. When I fell out of the event industry due to a worldwide pandemic, I considered my options of what to do with these skills. I set out on a mission to learn a new thing. A new thing called coding. This is where my journey as a software developer begins. I enrolled in Boise CodeWorks's 13-week immersive program and added some new skills to my repertoire. Through the course, I was able to apply my previous experience in marketing as well as my passion for art and creativity. When I'm not trying to center divs on webpages, you can find me in the great outdoors. I love to run, climb, bike, and roll in lush green grass.
